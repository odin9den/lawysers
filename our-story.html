Our Story

Our Director

Registered with the Quebec Bar since 2001, Louis Bolduc is a Canadian lawyer specialized in Immigration Law. After obtaining a Bachelor in History (Montreal/Toulouse, 1994), Mr. Bolduc simultaneously pursued a Master in History of Law and a Bachelor in Law (Montreal, 1997) and completed the first year of a PHD in History of Law (Quebec/Montpellier, 2000). In 2011, he obtained an Advanced Certificate in International Management at the Wagner Graduate School of Public Service (New York University).

Practicing as a research and legislation lawyer at the Quebec Justice Department, Mr. Bolduc developed an international comparative law paper for the lawyers of the Department: La justice d’ici et d’ailleurs. In 2001, he became an Immigration Officer for the Quebec Immigration Department. In this function, he worked on long-term assignments in North Africa, Middle East and Asia. Mr. Bolduc conducted hundreds of selection interviews for skilled workers and investors and served successively as Asia Desk Officer, Team Leader of the administrative appeal section and Expert Advisor for the business immigration politics and programs.
