Citizenship by Investment

Become a citizen of the World

Citizenship by Investment are programs designed by States to grant their citizenship to individuals and their families in exchange for an investment into the State’s economy. Citizenship by Investment programs benefit the host countries by boosting their economy, creating employment and improving the State’s fiscal performance and access to liquidities.

The advantages for you:

freedom of movement: a passeport granted for life offering visa-free travel to over 150 countries, including UK, Europe, HK, Singapore, and many more tax-friendly jurisdictions
fast processing (under 6 months), easy documentation different investment options, including donation (starting from USD100,000) and real estate investment (starting from USD200,000), with rental and financing opportunities
no one left behind: children, parents and grandparents can in included in the application and much more!

Citizenship by Investment is the perfect tool to become a citizen of the World, offer your family a beach retreat and ensure your children’s freedom for the rest of their lives. And in addition, you’ll contribute to create a sustainable future for your host country’s children.
